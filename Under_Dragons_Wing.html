UNDER DRAGON'S WING

In Under Dragon’s Wing, you are Treon, a young prince of Grendopolan and apprentice wizard — currently traveling in disguise with your two-hundred-year-old elven tutor and bodyguard, Rynath, who carries the enchanted long sword Glitteredge. Your kingdom is under attack: Rampillion, the Crimson Wizard, is raising an army of magical lava warriors, while his orc forces invade from the south. Now, in the backwater marketplace of Trigedium, you discover a mysterious ring and an ancient book of dragon summoning. Can you find a dragon ally to turn the tide — or will Rampillion’s power prove too great? Good luck and good adventuring!
